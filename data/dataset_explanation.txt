extracurricular_activities - Number of activities the student is involved in, not a likert scale:
depression - PHQ-9 assessment tool
anxiety_level - GAD-7 (Generalized Anxiety Disorder-7) scale
self_esteem - Assessed via Rosenberg Self-Esteem Scale, which is a standard 10-item questionnaire
mental_health_history - binary feature indicating whether the student has had any mental health issues in the past (0: No, 1: Yes)
stress_level - target column

blood_pressure - the frequency/intensity of blood-pressure–related symptoms caused by stress, rated on a 1–3 scale
